What is US FIRST Robotics?

     Since 1989, the FIRST program has strived "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology"(FIRST) and to help students all over the world gain interest and insight into the multiple fields of engineering and technology as well as real world experience by working together with other teams in coorpertition and with gracious professionalism to solve comlex problems.

What is FIRST FRC?

     FRC stands for FIRST Robotics Competition, and is the 9-12th grade compeitive league of FIRST. Along with Jr FLL(grade K-3), FLL(grades 4-8), and FTC(grades 7-12), it makes up one of the four main branches of the US FIRST organization.
